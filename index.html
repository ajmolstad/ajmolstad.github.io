I am an Associate Professor in the School of Statistics at the University of Minnesota. I also hold a graduate faculty appointment in Data Science. My primary research interests are in multivariate analysis, numerical optimization, statistical genetics and genomics, and more broadly, statistical and machine learning. Recently, I have focused on single-cell and spatial transcriptomic data analysis. I serve as Associate Editor for Data Science in Science and Journal of Computational and Graphical Statistics.
