I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Statistics and member of the Genetics Institute at the University of Florida. My primary research interests are in multivariate analysis, numerical optimization, tensor-valued data analysis, dimension reduction, and more broadly, statistical learning. Recently, I have been especially interested in applications in single cell data analysis. I serve as Associate Editor for Data Science in Science and Journal of Computational and Graphical Statistics.
